#miracle 
A city’s wells had been poisoned after a flood. [[Maya the Pure]] walked barefoot through the streets, cutting her feet upon the waylaid debris,  letting her blood fall into each well.

The water turned silver-white for one sunrise, then ran clear.

The sick recovered. Children stopped dying. The poor, who could not afford temple healers, saw her as a living mercy.

She spoke to the people:
> “No corruption is too deep to be drawn out.”
