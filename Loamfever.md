The sickness of the [[Farroot]]

## Living Stages:
* Cough
* Fever
* Hallucinations
	* Hearing soft voices from far away
	* Compulsively start walking towards a colony Fruit
* Death
	* White eyes
## Stages After Death:
#### False Recovery
* Becomes a Revenant
#### Loam-Scented Breath
* Your breath starts to smell of rot and mold.
* Bane on Persuade and Lie skills
* Double bane on Flirt
* **Fungal Breath** 
	* Main Action
	* Area, Magic
	* Power Roll + Presence
		* ≤ 11: 2 poison damage
		* 12-16: 4 poison damage
		* 17+: 6 poison damage
