The [[Hollow Crown]] attacked Capital roughly 150 years ago. #history 

[[Threxian Voss, the Heartless King]] created a weapon to harvest the souls of those nearby and use them to summon an Omen Dragon. This weapon was powered by the [[Mortal Coil]]'s innate magic. [[Threxian Voss, the Heartless King]] wanted to ensure that this artifact was destroyed since they saw it as a risk to their longevity. 

This included a multi-year endeavor of feeing the [[Order of the Closed Ear]] information that would lead them to try and thwart their plans, since the power of a #Saint is required to destroy the artifact. The Assault on [[Capital]] was the final part of their plan. [[Tovian, the Bellringer]] had been granted increasing amount of power as the threat of the [[Hollow Crown]] was becoming greater and greater.

[[Threxian Voss, the Heartless King]]'s plans worked, and [[Tovian, the Bellringer]] did destroy the artifact, but left [[Zorri]] [[Suspended in Time]] for over a hundred years as they were surrounded by the collected souls.
