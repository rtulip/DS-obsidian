A religious Zealot who believes in The Purity. They are a figurehead for the [[Followers of Maya]]. 

He has a son, [[BBEG's Son]], who has a birth defect/physical affliction. He faith leads him to believe his son has been damned by [[Adûn]], but holds onto hope that his soul is still pure and thus can be saved. He wishes to find the [[Location of the Soul]] such that it can be transplanted into a new [[Pure Host]].

His search will lead him to [[Lich|Lichdom]]

His beliefs are heresy within the [[Teachings of Maya]]
* Purity of the Souls deserves a Pure Vessel
* [[Adûn]]'s teachings of Life is twisted into the preservation of the soul at any cost
* [[Adûn]]'s teachings of Love has been twisted into a possessive parental obsession
