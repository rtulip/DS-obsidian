#cult 
A splinter organization of the [[Followers of Maya]] instructed by [[Barakat (BBEG)]]. All members carry the [[Last Breath's Mark]].

Each member of the order has been told that they are in some way [[Teachings of Maya|Impure]]. [[Barakat (BBEG)]] has convinced these that they have a chance to save themselves if they can aid in his search for the [[Location of the Soul]]. They have been sworn to secrecy, "lest the world hear of your self-made damnation".

This group has been doing the following:
* Robbing graves to study the dead
* Comparing the Bodies of the "Pure" vs the "Impure"
* Observing the body at the moment of death
* Experimenting on Living Subjects
