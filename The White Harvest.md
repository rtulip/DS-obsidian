#miracle
In a famine-struck province, [[Maya the Pure]] blessed fields ruined by blight. Overnight, the blackened grain turned pale gold, the soil softened, and the next harvest came in weeks rather than months.

But the miracle had a sign: every infected stalk became white as bone before crumbling into ash.

The people called it **the White Harvest**, proof that she could separate the clean from the corrupt.

She spoke to the people:

> “Impurity can be seen. Impurity can be burned away. What remains will feed us and makes us whole.”
