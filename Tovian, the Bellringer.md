#Saint 

A Saint the God [[Cavall]] and leader of the [[Order of the Closed Ear]]. He was tricked by [[Threxian Voss, the Heartless King]] to destroy the [[Mortal Coil]] which caused a [[Cataclysm]] in [[Capital]].

After the [[Cataclysm]], the people no longer saw Tovian as a #Saint (event if technically true). He was later referred to as the [[False Idol]], the [[Fifty-Year Saint]] and [[Dustbringer]].
